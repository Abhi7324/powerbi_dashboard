TRAFFIC INDICATORS ON I-94 - README

Overview
--------
This Power BI project analyzes hourly traffic volume on Interstate 94 to understand when and why heavy traffic occurs. The dashboard highlights trends across time, weather conditions, and temperature to help transportation planners make data-driven decisions.

Objectives
----------
- Identify major indicators of heavy traffic.
- Analyze monthly, weekly, and hourly traffic patterns.
- Understand the impact of weather and temperature on traffic volume.
- Provide actionable insights for congestion management.

Dataset
-------
Source: UCI Machine Learning Repository  
Features include:
- Date/Time  
- Temperature  
- Weather Conditions  
- Holiday Flag  
- Traffic Volume (hourly)

Dashboard Highlights
--------------------
1. Traffic Summary KPIs
   - Total, average, minimum, and maximum traffic volume

2. Trend Analysis
   - Traffic volume by Month
   - Traffic volume by Day of Week
   - Hourly rush-hour patterns

3. Distribution Insights
   - Histogram showing frequency distribution of traffic volumes

4. Weather Impact
   - Traffic by weather category
   - Scatter: Temperature vs Traffic Volume


Skills Demonstrated

- Interactive Visualizations  
- Storytelling with Data
